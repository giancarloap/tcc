Services

We offer an analysis of the browser history and creation of charts using Computer Graphics to show how productive you or your employees are when using the Internet.

Web Productivity Assessment

We analyze the browser history in order to make an assessment of how productive you or your employees are when using the internet.

Computer Graphics

Computer Graphics is used to show you how you spend your time on the Internet. After analyzing your browser history, Information Visualization techniques are used to make charts that show in what kind of sites you spend most of your time and if you are productive on the Web.

Web Productivity Improvement

After making a self-assessment based on the charts of your productivity, you can change your Internet habits, stop accessing the unproductive websites shown on the charts and so improve your Web productivity.

About

This project is part of the work to conclude the course of Computer Science at Pontifícia Universidade Católica do Rio Grande do Sul (Pontifical Catholic University of Rio Grande do Sul, Brazil). Part of the project is a research about Information Visualization which is an area of Computer Graphics. The other part of the project is making a software that uses the researched techniques of Information Visualization to make charts that show the productivity of the user when using the Internet based on the browser history to solve the problem of lack of productivity of employees or users in general.

  • November 2014

    Computer Graphics chosen to be subject of the work to conclude the course of Computer Science.

    Information visualization, which is an area of Computer Graphics is chosen as topic of the college conclusion work!

  • March 2015

    Research supervisor gives the suggestion of topic

    The topic is making a browser extension that analyzes the browser history and make charts to show the user where he spends most of his time in the Internet to improve his productivity!

  • June 2015

    First part of the college conclusion work is concluded

    The first part included a research about Information Visualization Techniques, and some tests with charts based on real data of the browser history!

  • November 2015

    Part two will be concluded

    The second part will include the implementation of the ideas proposed on part one, along with a research paper with the theoretical foundation for the software, and research results!
